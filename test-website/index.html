Thorsheim Kindred

This webpage is a practice project dedicated to my dear brothers and our Thorsheim Kindred.

Back in 2021 there were only 3 of us: Eduard, Donald and Myself. At the time... we weren't calling ourselves a kindred.

After much thought and consideration we decided we wanted to formalize our gatherings and bonds of kinship in the form of a kindred.

A year later we were lucky enough to add another brother, Joe, to the kindred.

Thorsheim Kindred is a gathering of likeminded folk that enjoy:

  • Honoring The Old Norse Gods
  • Honoring The Ancestors
  • Learning about the History and Traditions of the Old Norse
